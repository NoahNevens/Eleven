Rules

  • Collect all trophies to pass level, pass all levels to win game
  • Move with WASD, click buttons
  • Lose when health reaches 0
  • Draw types (colors) using "Draw" or by collecting them in arena
  • Use types to attack, block, and cast abilities (attacks and abilities cost 1 energy)
  • When you bump into enemies you can attack them and they can attack you
  • You can attack enemies with any type except their own (e.g. red enemies cannot be attacked with red)
  • Different types have different effects when you attack with them
  • You can block an attack with the same type (e.g. a blue enemy can be blocked with blue)
  • If you don't block you take damage
  • Abilities can be cast when you have the necessary color schemes, read descriptions
  • You have a limited number of draws that increases every 4 moves (draws also cost 1 energy)
  • If you move 12 consecutive times without collecting a trophy you will start taking damage
  • Types have a 7% chance of spawning on the arena with each move
  • Guard enemies stand still and spawn at the beginning of each level
  • Chaser enemies move toward the main player with each move
  • Chaser enemies have a ???% chance of spawning on the arena with each move
  • Enemies get tougher and more unique with each level
  • When you collect a trophy the next trophy will spawn at another place on the arena
  • "Storm" destroys all enemies on arena but costs 10 * (current level) health and energy
  • "Cast" allows you to cast an ability after clicking it
  • When you reach 0 energy you can't really do much
